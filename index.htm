About ℹ️
Welcome to my testing laboratory! This page serves as a space for
exploration, experimentation, and continuous learning. As a dedicated
QA Analyst with a strong foundation in manual testing, I also have a
keen interest in JavaScript for test automation. What started as a
simple display of my Cypress scripts has naturally evolved. Each
update reflects the progression of an iterative approach and gives
a comprehensive insight into my testing skills and QA philosophy.

Curious about the design choice of this page? It was inspired by
and a nod to the charm of 90s websites. I appreciate the creativity
and individuality that characterized many websites from that era. I
wanted to bring back the simpler times of the internet and add a bit
of nostalgia and handmade style to it. Imagine there is background
midi music 🎵 playing for the full experience.
